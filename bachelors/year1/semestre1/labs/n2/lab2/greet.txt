Here is program that gives you opportunity doing some operations with
geometric figures (polygon, circle).

What you can do(after commant follow instructions):
1)Input some figure
    inp
2)Get square of figure
    sqr
3)Get perimeter of figure
    per
4)Get lengths of sides of figure
    sidelen

Before doing following you should enter combination (following the instructions)
5)Intersect
    inter
6)Union
    union
7)Difference
    diff
8)Symmetric difference
    symd
